What is a Penguin?

Penguins are small animals that are mostly known to be seen in Antartica. Types of penguins may include the Emperor Penguin, the African Penguin, the King Penguin and many more! Penguins love to curl up with each other to keep themselves warm. Though penguins cannot fly they can dive and even catch their own fish.

Penguins vs. Puffins

  • Although both birds have a similar black and white feather color and a love of fish, penguins belong to the family Spheniscidae, and puffins belong to the family Alcidae.
  • Though puffins can fly and penguins cannot overall penguins are cuter.
